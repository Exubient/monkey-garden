Bark - No One to Someone

Bark redefines the way you make new friends nearby. It removes labels of age, gender, or social-identity, and lets you become a dog in our playground.

All you have to do is simply push your bark to people nearby. When they hear your bark, they will bark back. You don't need to wait for their comments or likes, or for someone to swipe so you can find a match. Just simply Bark and connect instantly.

You can build relationships with other dogs (people) in our playground by barking to each other, or by sending short messages. Through these simple and efficient methods, Bark can help you make connections and introduce new friends to your life. From "no-one to someone", that's the first step of making friends.
